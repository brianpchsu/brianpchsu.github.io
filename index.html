About Me

Hi! I'm Brian Hsu — a Software Engineering Manager in Silicon Valley with a story that didn't start in a computer science classroom. My non-traditional pivot into tech has given me a firsthand understanding of what it takes to navigate career transitions, scale engineering teams, and lead with empathy. That journey also sparked a passion for community building: I run Pivot Software Engineer in Silicon Valley, where I share raw, first-hand career observations, and founded Taiwanese Referral Network — a referral network uniting over 300 overseas Taiwanese professionals to break down information barriers and open doors in the global tech industry.

關於我

你好！我是 Brian Hsu，目前於矽谷擔任 Software Engineering Manager。我的科技之路並非從 CS 本科開始——正是這段半路出家的歷程，讓我對跨領域轉職的挑戰有了深刻的切身體會，也形塑了我在帶領工程團隊時更具韌性與同理心的視角。我熱衷於把自己踩過的坑與累積的觀察，化為對他人有價值的資源：透過半路工程師在矽谷，我持續記錄矽谷職場的第一手洞察；而我發起的歹晚郎內推互助網絡，集結超過 300 位海外台灣人，致力於打破資訊不對稱，幫助更多優秀人才在國際舞台找到屬於自己的位置。
